Friends With Beer



In these uncertain economic times you need to keep your friends close, and your beer even closer.

















This app quickly identifies your pals who are most likely to have your favorite brew on ice. Check the traffic conditions around your friend's house, then dial or email them to let them know that you're coming over.

No friends? No problem! Our app contains a video of "virtual" friends drinking, so you'll never need to drink alone again!
